About FHS Survey Forms

FHS Survey Forms is a simple web application.

Our Mission

At FHS Survey Forms, we are passionate about ensuring customer satisfaction. Our platform empowers technitians, researchers and customers in ensuring good relationships as well as maintaining and protecting their equipment through collaborative surveys and data-driven insights.

What We Do

  • Provide a user-friendly web app for recording and sharing customer equipment observations.
  • Enable crowd-sourced equipment surveys to track machine distribution and health.
  • Offer data visualization tools to help researchers and policymakers make informed conservation descisions.
  • Foster a community of technitians, researchers, and citizen scientists dedicated to protecting our customers equipment / machines.

Our Team

We are a group of technitians, developers, and data scientits united by a shared love for customer satisfaction. Our expertise in maintenance, software development, and AI-driven analytics ensures that our platform is both scientifically robust and easy to use.

Why Choose Us ?

  • Accurate Data Collection - Advanced tools for precise equipment identification.
  • Global Collaboration - Connect researchers and volunteers worldwide.
  • Sustainable Focus - Contributing to better customer relationship and satisfaction.
